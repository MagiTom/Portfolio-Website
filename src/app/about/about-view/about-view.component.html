about me

Hello!
My name's Magda.
I am a graduate of postgraduate studies "Front-End developer with Angular" which I finished with the best posibility grade I wanted to learn as much as possible to start made my own ideas for projects.

My adventure with programming started when I discovered that I wanted do something more creative and valuable than before. I watched with envy the work of graphic designers, programmers, architects, seeing how they participate in a production of something useful and pretty. I was wondering what can I do to change my job and then, step by step I started to learn new skills.

At the beginning of this road I was learning Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and XD in a computer graphics academy. In this course I also got to know HTML and CSS. After that, I noticed that I wanted do something more interactive and that it would be nice to make some apps or websites. And so I decided to try programming. My friends always told me I had an analytical mind, and that programming should be good for me. I covered many topics really fast since they were a lot of fun to me, and so I put even more effort into it.

I am a calm person, but with a lot sense of humor. I have hobbies that are not typical for a woman, for example I like fishing and programming The fact is that when I become interested in something I always strive to be the best at it, thus I work a lot to achieve the best results (and a lot of tasteful fish ).
